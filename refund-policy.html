Refund Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Training Services

Learn With Abinash offers personalized 1-on-1 training programs. We want you to be fully satisfied with your learning experience. Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both parties.

2. Free Demo Class

We offer a free demo class before enrollment so you can experience our teaching style and decide if it's the right fit for you. We strongly recommend attending the demo class before making any payment.

3. Refund Eligibility

  • Before training starts: Full refund if you cancel before the first paid session.
  • Within the first week: Refund of the remaining balance (minus fees for sessions already conducted) if you are not satisfied after the first 2 sessions.
  • After the first week: Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss your situation.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Sessions that have already been conducted
  • Study materials and resources that have been shared
  • Certificate issuance fees (if applicable)

5. Free Content

Our practice exams, YouTube videos, and website content are provided entirely free of charge. No refund policy applies to free content.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through any of the following channels:

  • WhatsApp: Message us on WhatsApp (fastest response)
  • Email: Learn.With.Abinash@gmail.com

Please include your name, the training program, and the reason for your refund request. We will respond within 2 business days.

7. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please reach out:

  • Email: Learn.With.Abinash@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: Message us on WhatsApp
